BanaSafe

Safe, Confidential GBV Reporting via SMS

About BanaSafe

BanaSafe is a service that allows individuals in Botswana to report gender-based violence confidentially via SMS.

How It Works

Send an SMS with REPORT [your location] to our shortcode. You will receive an automated confirmation.

Contact

Email: support@banasafe.org

Emergency: If you are in immediate danger, call 999.
